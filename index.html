  • wheel alignment

    Alignment refers to an adjustment of a vehicle’s suspension – the system that connects a vehicle to its wheels. It is not an adjustment of the tires or wheels themselves. The key to proper alignment is adjusting the angles of the tires which affects how they make contact with the road.

  • transmission repair and replacement

    Transmission Is Slipping: If you are experiencing transmission slipping, it will feel like you are driving in one gear and then it will change for no reason. It might also make a whining noise when this happens. If this happens to you when you are driving, there is an issue with your transmission and you need to get it fixed as soon as possible. This could occur because your transmission fluid is too low or a lack of maintenance. This can cause wear and tear on your entire transmission system. Eventually, this will lead to worse problems and you will need to repair your transmission.
